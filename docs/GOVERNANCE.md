# Governance & Roles

The NeuroToken ZK Health System™ requires multi-layer governance:

- **Lionking BioTech™ / Lionking Holding AS**
  - IP ownership and long-term vision
  - Coordination of research, pilots and partnerships

- **Clinical & rehabilitation partners**
  - Control over clinical processes and patient relationships
  - Responsibility for medical quality and ethical approval

- **Technical partners (AI, ZK, infrastructure)**
  - Implement, harden and maintain the technical stack
  - Participate in security and privacy reviews

- **Advisory board**
  - Combine legal, medical, cryptography and patient-representative perspectives
