# Roadmap 2025–2030 (Draft)

- **2025**
  - Concept consolidation and patent filings
  - Initial legal and regulatory analysis
  - Early partner conversations (healthcare, ZK, crypto infra)

- **2026**
  - Prototype designs and simulation tools (no live patients)
  - Alignment with Hjernensprer.no and BrainSays.com pilots
  - Refinement of token and incentive models

- **2027–2028**
  - Controlled pilots with synthetic or heavily anonymised data
  - External audits and ethical reviews
  - Preparation of public-facing whitepaper and governance framework

- **2029–2030**
  - Scaling strategy subject to regulatory green light and partner readiness
  - Potential separation between foundation, operating entities and infrastructure providers
